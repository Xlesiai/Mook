# Mook
This Android application offers a user-friendly interface designed to simplify the conversion of 
book PDFs into audio files and audio files into text seamlessly. Its intuitive functionality allows 
users to effortlessly transform their favorite literary works into accessible audio formats, 
enabling convenient listening experiences on the go. Similarly, it converts audio files into 
accurate textual representations, making it an invaluable tool for those seeking a quick and 
efficient way to transcribe spoken content. With its straightforward approach and versatile 
capabilities, this app redefines convenience in accessing and repurposing literary and auditory 
content.
